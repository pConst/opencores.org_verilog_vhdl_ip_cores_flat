Description

Here i am trying to demonstrate my idea, that Russell's paradox of set theory can be solved by computer simulation in discrete time. It leads to oscillating sets, meaning theese sets automatically become existent and then disappear over time by their definition. Furthermore i try to describe it in such an event driven way, where theoretically the time between the events can be infinitely short...
